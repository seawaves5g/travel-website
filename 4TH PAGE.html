FOOD

 

 

 Tarkarli and the surrounding areas are known for their delicious Malvani cuisine, which predominantly features a variety of flavorful seafood dishes. When in Tarkarli, here are some of the best Malvani dishes you should try and recommended places where you can enjoy them:

1. Malvani Fish Curry: This spicy and tangy coconut-based curry is a staple in Malvani cuisine. You can find excellent Malvani fish curry at local restaurants and beach shacks in Tarkarli.

2. Bombil Fry (Bombay Duck Fry): Bombil, also known as Bombay Duck, is a popular fish in the region. It's often marinated, coated in spices, and fried until crispy. Many local eateries serve delicious Bombil Fry.

3. Solkadhi: Solkadhi is a refreshing drink made with coconut milk, kokum (a fruit native to the region), and spices. It's a perfect accompaniment to spicy Malvani dishes. You can find Solkadhi in most restaurants in Tarkarli.

4. Prawns Masala: Malvan is renowned for its prawns, and you'll find various prawn dishes on the menu. Prawns masala, cooked with aromatic spices and herbs, is a must-try for seafood lovers.

5. Clams Sukka:Clams sukka is a dry dish made with clams, coconut, and Malvani spices. It's flavorful and pairs well with rice or roti. Look for it in local seafood restaurants.

6. Kombdi Vade:Kombdi vade is a traditional Malvani chicken curry served with puri-like fried bread made from rice flour. It's a hearty and flavorful dish often served during special occasions.

7. Modak:Modak is a popular sweet dish made during festivals. It's a steamed dumpling filled with jaggery, coconut, and nuts. You might find modak in local sweet shops or restaurants, especially during Ganesh Chaturthi.

As for the best places to enjoy these dishes, you can try local eateries, beachside shacks, and restaurants in Tarkarli and nearby towns like Malvan. Many guesthouses and resorts also offer authentic Malvani cuisine to their guests. Don't hesitate to ask locals for recommendations, as they can often point you to hidden gems where you can savor the best Malvani flavors.
